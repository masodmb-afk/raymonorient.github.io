About Us

Raymon Orient Trading FZE is a UAE-based trading company specialized in metals and non‑ferrous materials, providing transparent and well‑documented trade operations for industrial clients. We focus on reliability, compliance, and long‑term partnerships across the GCC and Asia.

Our Focus

  • Long‑term cooperation with verified regional producers

Our Services

  • Complete documentation — invoice, packing list, COA, BL
  • Coordination with inspection or surveyors when required
  • Payment terms: primarily LC‑based transactions
  • Continuous follow‑up from order to delivery
